# Identifying User Requirements

### Visitor/Students

Functional Requirements:

1. Exploring the Campus Map \- Students and visitors can navigate the map to locate buildings, classrooms, offices, and facilities  
2. Search and Filter \- They can search for specific rooms, departments, or events using keywords or filters  
3. View Room and Event Details \- Users can click on markers to see room availability, building hours, or view upcoming events happening  
4. Book Available Rooms \- Limited to Students, can request study room, group spaces which are all subject to availability   
5. Access Event listings \- Students and Visitors can view a list of upcoming campus events and view details such as time, location, and dates.  
6. Notifications \- Students may receive updates on room bookings, changes to events, or campus alerts

Non-Functional Requirements

1. User-Friendly Interface \- The map should be clear, and have simple navigation tools and icons that are understandable for first time users  
2. Responsiveness \- The system must be useable under regular conditions on all modern browsers  
3. Performance \- Map loading and search results should be quick and reasonable (under 2 seconds) under regular conditions on all modern browsers  
4. Data Accuracy \- The platform should have accurate information on building locations, hours of operation and events taking place and must be kept up-to-date to maintain trust and useability

### Faculty/Staff

Functional Requirements:

1. View Room Availability \- Faculty and Staff can view classrooms, labs, and meeting rooms that are available in real time  
2. Book and Manage Rooms \- They may be able to reserve rooms for lectures, office hours, department events or other meetings. They may also cancel or view their bookings  
3. Change Department Information \- Specific people may be authorized by admins to to update department-related info such as office hours, staff directories and contact information  
4. Posting Department Events \- Authorized users may add or modify academic or department events that are on the map  
5. Priority Booking \- Faculty and Staff will get higher priority than students and visitors for academic and administrative room bookings

Non-Functional Requirements

1. Ease of Use \- The system should be quick and intuitive, requiring minimal steps for booking  
2. Reliability \- The booking information and system must be real-time to avoid multiple-bookings and maintain a high up-time  
3. Security \- Role-based access for ensuring only authorized staff can change department data  
4. Accessibility \- The system interface should be responsive and operate normally on all modern browsers  
5. Performance \- The map interactions and bookings should load in a reasonable time under regular conditions for best usability

### Administrators

Functional Requirements

1. Manage User Roles \- Admins can create, modify or remove accounts and assign roles to control access  
2. Approve and Oversee Bookings \- Admins can view, approve, and deny building booking requests submitted by students, faculty or staff ensuring fair and organized space usage  
3. Edit and Update Campus Data \- Admins can change building details, hours and manage available facilities across campus  
4. Manage Events \- They can add, edit, or remove events listed ensuring accurate data, and events following guidelines  
5. Monitor Usage \- They can monitor and track stats such as booking frequency, event attendance, and user activity on platform for performance utilization  
6. System Maintenance \- Admins can disable or restrict usage on platform for various reasons such as regular maintenance, construction or emergencies

Non-Functional Requirements:

1. Security and Privacy \- The system should handle sensitive data and must secure it safely in a database only accessible by authorized personnel  
2. Reliability \- The platform should maintain a 99% runtime for regular maintenance and to prevent data loss through regular backups  
3. Auditability \- All admin actions should be logged for accountability and troubleshooting  
4. Ease of Management \- Admin dashboards should be intuitive, such as filters, search tools, and analytics for quick summary for oversight  
5. Performance \- Admin updates to system should reflect immediately in real-time  
6. Compliance \- Platform should adhere to institutional data policies and privacy standards

### Event Coordinators

Functional Requirements:

1. Create Events \- The Event Coordinators will be able to create events, set their locations, date and time, and provide descriptions.   
2. Manage Existing Events \- The Event Coordinators will be able to manage events and update their locations, date and time, and descriptions.

Non-Functional Requirements:

1. Ease of Use \- The system should be quick and intuitive, requiring minimal steps for booking  
2. Reliability \- The booking information and system must be real-time to avoid multiple-bookings and maintain a high up-time  
3. Security \- Role-based access for ensuring only authorized staff can change department data  
4. Accessibility \- The system interface should be responsive and operate normally on all modern browsers  
5. Performance \- The map interactions and bookings should load in a reasonable time under regular conditions for best usability
